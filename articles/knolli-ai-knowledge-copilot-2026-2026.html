Knolli.ai: Turning Your Expertise Into a Paid AI Knowledge Copilot (2026)

If you've spent years building up specialized knowledge in a niche, Knolli.ai offers a way to package that expertise into something people pay to access — without having to build any of the underlying AI infrastructure yourself.

- You upload your knowledge base: PDFs, internal docs, guides, spreadsheets, whatever captures your expertise - Knolli wraps it in a private AI chat tool (a "Knowledge Copilot") that answers questions using only your material - You set the monetization: subscription, pay-per-use, or a hybrid - Knolli handles the infrastructure, hosting, and data protection - A free starting tier exists for small-scale creators testing the waters

This model works best for people who already have: - A body of written material (courses, guides, internal SOPs, research) - An audience that already trusts their expertise and would pay for faster/deeper access to it - No interest in building or maintaining their own AI/RAG infrastructure

It's a poor fit if you don't have existing content — this is a productization tool, not a content-creation tool.

A copilot charging even $10-20/month with 20-30 subscribers is a meaningful passive-ish income stream once set up, since the infrastructure and Q&A handling is automated after initial setup. The bottleneck is audience size and trust, not the tool itself.
