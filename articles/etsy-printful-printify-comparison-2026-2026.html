Comprehensive breakdown of Etsy, Printful, and Printify for scaling a POD business. Compare setup costs, per-unit margins, design control, automation, and which platform wins for different business sizes.

Etsy vs Printful vs Printify: Which POD Platform Wins in 2026?

| Factor | Etsy Shop | Printful | Printify | |---|---|---|---| | Setup Cost | Free shop + listing fees | Free | Free | | Per-Unit Margin | Platform takes 6-8% fee | ~40-60% profit | ~45-65% profit | | Design Control | Limited templates | Full design control | Full design control | | Automation | Basic integrations | Shopify/WooCommerce | 100+ integrations | | Scaling Speed | Slow (manual listings) | Fast (bulk upload) | Fastest (API-first) |

Etsy: The Proven Marketplace Etsy is a ready-made audience — no marketing budget needed. But the 6-8% fees and review process slow scaling.

Printful: The All-in-One Printful handles design, printing, shipping. Great for beginners, but their margin structure rewards volume heavily.

Printify: The Hacker's Choice Printify's open platform lets you connect to any store (Shopify, Wix, WooCommerce, even custom apps). Best for automation and scaling.

The Real Decision - No technical skills? Start on Etsy for audience, then move to Printful. - Want to scale fast? Printify + Shopify is the fastest path to $5K/month. - Building a brand? Printify to your own store (WooCommerce/Wix).
