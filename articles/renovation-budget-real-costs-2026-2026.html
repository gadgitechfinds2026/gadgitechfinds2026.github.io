Renovation Budgeting: How to Avoid the "Only $5k Over" Lie

The Cost Categories You'll Actually Have

Structural & Foundation - Foundation cracks, settling, water intrusion: $2,000-15,000+ (varies wildly) - Roof repair/replacement: $8,000-20,000+ (depends on size and materials) - Framing rot, termites: $1,000-10,000+ (discovery kills timelines)

Systems (Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC) - Full electrical rewire: $3,000-8,000 - Plumbing system (partial upgrade): $2,000-6,000 - HVAC replacement: $4,000-12,000 - These are not negotiable. You can't have a house without them.

Finishes (What You See) - Drywall, tape, mud: $1,000-3,000 per property - Paint: $1,500-4,000 (labor heavy) - Flooring: $2,000-8,000 (huge variation: laminate vs. luxury vinyl vs. tile) - Kitchen cabinets: $3,000-10,000 (cheap route = contractor-grade, refaced, or Habitat bins) - Bathroom tile/fixtures: $1,500-5,000 per bathroom

Contingency (The Honest Buffer) - 20% of total budget, minimum. - If you estimate $50k, add $10k. - If you estimate $100k, add $20k. - Contractors will tell you 10% is enough. They're wrong or they're padding their profit.
