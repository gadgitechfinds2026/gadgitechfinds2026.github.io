How to Start a Profitable Print-on-Demand Side Hustle in 2026

Print-on-demand (POD) has become a legitimate path to part-time income. This guide walks you through the process with real numbers and actionable steps.

POD is a business model where: - You design or curate products (hoodies, mugs, stickers, etc.) - A printing partner manufactures items only when ordered - You set your markup; partners handle fulfillment and shipping - You earn the difference between your price and the production cost

Earnings potential: $500-$5,000/month for a part-time operator with focused niche.

Success in POD depends on niche clarity:

Good niches (validated, repeat buyers): - Tech/developer culture (narrow, affluent, loyal) - Fitness/gym communities - Pet lover merchandise - Hobby-specific apparel (gaming, reading, etc.)
