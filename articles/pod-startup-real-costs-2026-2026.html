Print-on-Demand Startup Costs: The Real Numbers (2026)

Every POD guide online says "start with $0." That's technically true but wildly misleading. Here's what actually costs money and what doesn't.

Printify Account: $0 - Free tier includes unlimited designs, unlimited products, 5 sales channels. - You only pay when someone buys. - Printify takes a cut (varies by product: $2–8 per item for apparel).

Etsy Shop (if using Etsy as your sales channel): $0 to start, $0.20 per listing (renews every 3 months unless sold). - 100 listings × $0.20 = $20 listing fee. - But if you sell, it pays for itself in 1–2 sales.

Design Tools: - Canva Pro: Free tier works for POD (limited templates), $13/month for pro. - Photoshop alternative: Use Krita (free) or GIMP (free). Harder learning curve.

Social Media: $0 (Reddit, Tumblr, Pinterest, TikTok are free).
