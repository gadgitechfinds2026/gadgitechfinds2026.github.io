Beehiiv vs Substack: Newsletter Monetization Battle 2026

Newsletters are one of the most reliable passive income streams of 2026. Both Beehiiv and Substack dominate this space, but they take very different approaches.

- Beehiiv: Best for aggressive growth and monetization. Ad network pays creators instantly. Free tier. Founder-friendly. - Substack: Best for writers who want full control and direct reader relationships. Higher payouts per paid subscriber. Sophisticated analytics.

Pricing - Free: Unlimited subscribers, basic email sends. $0. - Premium: $15/month for advanced features (AI, templates, analytics).

Monetization Options - Ad Network: Beehiiv matches your newsletter with relevant ads. You earn 75% of ad revenue, Beehiiv takes 25%. - Paid Subscriptions: You set the price (typically $5-20/month). Beehiiv takes a small percentage. - Affiliate Network: Recommend products directly to subscribers. Earn 10-50% commissions. - Boosts: Referral program that pays you for every 10 subscriptions you refer.

Strengths - Ad network income from day 1: Unlike Substack, you don't need 100 paid subscribers to start earning. - Growth tools: Referral system, "Sponsor" integration, viral loops built in. - Free tier is actually free: No paywall on growth. - Affiliate program: Earn from recommending other tools. - Founder-friendly: Responsive support, regular feature launches.
