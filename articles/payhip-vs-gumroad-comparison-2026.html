Payhip vs Gumroad: Which Platform is Right for Your Digital Products?

If you're selling digital products—courses, guides, ebooks, templates—you've probably heard of Payhip and Gumroad. Both offer free tiers and low barriers to entry. But which one is right for you?

- Payhip: Best for sellers who want unlimited products on a free plan, lower fees (5%), and more control over branding. - Gumroad: Best for creators with large audiences, those who want built-in community features, and people who prioritize simplicity.

Payhip: The Feature-Rich Option

Pricing - Free tier: Unlimited products, unlimited downloads. 5% fee per sale + payment processing fees (~2.2%). - Pro tier: $29/month for additional features.

Strengths - No product limits: Sell as many products as you want on the free tier. - Lower transaction fees: 5% is competitive (Gumroad charges 10%). - Affiliate program: Built-in affiliate marketing features. - Membership support: Create recurring subscription products. - Email automations: Follow-ups and upsells.
