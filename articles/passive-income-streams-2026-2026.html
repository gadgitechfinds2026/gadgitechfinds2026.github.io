Ultimate Guide to Passive Income Streams in 2026

Passive income is the holy grail of financial independence, but not all passive income streams are created equal. Some require significant upfront work, while others demand ongoing maintenance. Let's break down the most legitimate options for 2026.

True passive income earns money with minimal ongoing effort after the initial setup. However, most "passive" streams require some maintenance: - Updates and refreshes - Marketing and promotion - Customer service or community management

Expect to invest 20-40 hours initially for most streams, then 2-5 hours/month maintaining them.
