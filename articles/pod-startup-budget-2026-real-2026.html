POD Startup Budget Reality Check (2026)

Option C: Hybrid (Recommended) - Buy design templates (Etsy templates): $3-10 each × 10 = $50 - Modify in Canva: $13/month - Commission 2-3 custom designs for hero products: $100-400 - Cost: $150-400 + ongoing $13/month
