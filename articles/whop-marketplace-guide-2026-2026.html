Whop Marketplace: Free Digital Sales Platform

Why Whop? Whop is built for creators selling niche digital products. Zero listing fees, zero monthly fees, no exclusivity agreements. You own your customer data. The platform takes a ~3% commission on the purchase amount (industry standard), but there are no other hidden fees.

What You Can Sell - Discord server access (memberships, communities, education channels) - Digital tools and software (scripts, bots, design templates) - Digital files (Notion templates, spreadsheets, guides) - Courses and educational content - Affiliate-style products (recommending other services with a link)

Platform Economics - Commission: ~3% per sale (Whop + payment processing combined) - Payout: Direct to your bank account or crypto wallet - No monthly fee: Sell 0 items or 1,000 — same cost - Customer emails: You own them (not locked into Whop)

Getting Started 1. Sign up at whop.com/sell (1 min) 2. Create a 'product' — either upload a file, set up a Discord invite, or link to a service 3. Set your price and payment terms (one-time, subscription, pay-what-you-want) 4. Share the link — Whop handles payment collection and delivery 5. Payouts happen weekly to your bank account
