Payhip Free Tier Guide: Zero-Cost Digital Product Sales

Why Payhip in 2026? Payhip's free tier is genuinely unlimited — unlimited products, unlimited sales, zero monthly fees. You only pay 5% when a customer buys. Unlike Gumroad (which reserves features for paid tiers), Payhip gives you the full product suite on day one.

Core Features on the Free Tier - Product Types: Digital downloads, courses, video hosting, memberships, services - Payment Processing: Stripe + PayPal integration (you set up your own accounts; Payhip doesn't take a cut of payment processing) - Email Delivery: Automatic digital download delivery on purchase - License Keys: Digital product licensing system (useful for software sales) - Email List: Built-in list of customers who bought your products

Setup Steps 1. Sign up at payhip.com (free, no credit card required) 2. Connect your Stripe or PayPal account for payment processing 3. Create your first product (digital file upload or course content) 4. Set your price and launch — customers buy immediately 5. Earnings hit your Stripe/PayPal account after the sale (minus 5%)

Real Income Example - Sell 10 digital guides at $29 each = $290 revenue - Payhip takes 5% = $14.50 - Stripe/PayPal takes ~2.9% + $0.30 = ~$9.20 - You keep: ~$266.30 from 10 sales

Best for These Products - PDF guides, templates, Notion setups - Video courses (even short 3-10 minute guides) - eBooks and written content - Software tools and code snippets - Membership access to exclusive content
